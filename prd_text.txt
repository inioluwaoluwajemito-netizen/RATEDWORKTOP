RatedWorktops – Project Requirements Document (PRD)
Version 1.0This document defines the functional and non-functional requirements for the RatedWorktops web and mobile application.
1. Project Overview
RatedWorktops is an AI-powered web and mobile application that allows customers to upload a photo of their kitchen and visualise different stone materials on worktops, splashbacks and kitchen islands.
The application replaces existing surfaces in the uploaded image with selected materials from a library of stone brands and colours.
2. Business Objectives
Help customers visualise worktops before purchasing.
Promote stone brands and collections.
Generate customer engagement and leads.
Offer both free and subscription-based business models.
3. Platforms
Web application (desktop and mobile browser).
iOS application.
Android application.
4. User Roles
Customer – Upload images, generate visualisations, save projects, download and share images.
Administrator – Manage users, subscriptions, brands, categories and analytics.
5. Customer Features
Registration via email/password or Google Sign-In.
Email verification and password reset.
Upload kitchen images (JPG, PNG . . . etc).
Browse stone brands and stone colours.
Select a material and generate an AI visualisation.
Unlimited generation of visualisations, or up to (a specific number per 24 hours)
Download generated images.
Share images via WhatsApp, Email, Facebook, Instagram and Copy Link.
All shared and downloaded images should include a subtle 'Created with RatedWorktops' watermark and company logo.
Customers can save only 1–2 projects on their account to minimise storage costs.
When the save limit is reached, users must delete an existing project before saving another (or suggest another way)
6. Image Storage Policy
Unsaved generated images are stored temporarily and automatically deleted after a configurable period (default 48 hours).
Only saved projects remain in the customer account.
7. Monetisation & Subscription
The platform must support both free and paid operation modes.
Free Mode: Unlimited use with subscriptions disabled.
Paid Mode: Free credits enabled and subscription required after credits are used.
Support monthly and annual subscription plans.
8. Admin Portal
Dashboard showing total users, active users, visualisations, downloads, shares and subscription revenue.
Search, suspend and delete customers.
View customer activity and credit usage.
Reset customer credits if required.
Enable or disable free credits with a simple ON/OFF toggle.
Enable or disable subscriptions with a simple ON/OFF toggle.
Configure number of free credits and subscription pricing.
9. Stone Library Management
Create, edit and delete stone brands.
Each brand may contain approximately 100 stone colours.
Bulk upload stone colours and images.
Enable or disable brands and colours.
10. Categories
Stone brands and colours can be organised into categories.
Examples: Marble, Granite, Quartz, Quartzite, Porcelain, Sintered Stone, Limestone, Onyx and Travertine.
Admin can create, edit, delete, enable, disable and reorder categories.
Search and filter by category, brand and stone name.
